Quick & Easy Ginger Tea Recipe for Sore Throat Relief

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ginger: Peel the ginger root and slice it into thin pieces. Use a grater if you prefer a stronger flavor.
  2. Boil the Water: Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan. Add the ginger slices and reduce to a simmer.
  3. Simmer: Let the ginger steep in the simmering water for 10-15 minutes to extract its goodness.
  4. Strain: Remove the ginger pieces by straining the tea into a cup.
  5. Add Flavor: Stir in the honey and lemon juice while the tea is still warm. Add a pinch of cinnamon or turmeric for an extra health boost.
  6. Enjoy: Sip the warm tea slowly and feel the soothing relief in your throat.
