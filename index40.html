1. Lemon Cucumber Water

This detox drink is a powerhouse for hydration and cleansing. The combination of lemon and cucumber helps to flush out toxins from your body, while keeping you refreshed and hydrated throughout the day.

  • Lemon: A natural detoxifier that boosts your immune system and aids in digestion.
  • Cucumber: Known for its high water content, cucumber helps hydrate your body and reduce bloating. It also has anti-inflammatory properties that promote healthy skin.

How to Make:

Add a few slices of cucumber and a couple of lemon wedges to a glass of water. Let it sit for about 10 minutes to infuse, and enjoy throughout the day!

Tip: Drink this first thing in the morning to kickstart your metabolism and cleanse your system. üçã

Apple cider vinegar (ACV) is a popular ingredient for detoxing and weight loss. It helps balance blood sugar levels, curb cravings, and improve digestion. This drink is a great way to start your day or enjoy before meals to support weight loss.

  • Apple Cider Vinegar: Helps detoxify the liver, improve digestion, and curb appetite.
